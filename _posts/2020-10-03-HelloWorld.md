**Equations**

The _Navier-Stokes Equations_

$$
\begin{split}
   &\frac{\partial \vec{u}}{\partial t} + \left(\vec{u}\cdot\nabla\right)\vec{u} = -\frac{1}{\rho}\nabla p + \nu\nabla^2\vec{u} + \vec{f},\\
   &\nabla\cdot\vec{u} = 0.
\end{split}
$$

Where $\vec{u}$ is the velocity field and $p$ is the pressure field.
